Naturinex Privacy Policy

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: July 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Naturinex ("we", "our", or "us"), a product operated under DBA by Cien Rios LLC, collects, uses, and discloses your information.

AI Technology Disclosure

Important: Naturinex uses artificial intelligence (AI) technology powered by OpenAI and Google Gemini to analyze medications and suggest natural alternatives. AI-generated content:

  • May contain inaccuracies or errors
  • Should not be relied upon as sole source of health information
  • Must be verified with healthcare professionals
  • Is for educational and informational purposes only

Information We Collect:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address via account creation or third-party logins (Google OAuth)
  • Health-Related Data:
    • Medication photos and scan results (anonymized)
    • Search queries for medications and supplements
    • Scan history and saved analyses
    • Health preferences and goals (if provided)
  • Device Information:
    • Device type, operating system, and version
    • Unique device identifiers
    • IP address for security purposes
    • App usage analytics and crash reports
  • Camera and Photos:
    • Camera access for medication scanning
    • Photo library access to select existing images
    • Images are processed locally when possible

How We Use Your Information:

  • To deliver AI-driven scan insights and app functionality
  • To process medication images and provide natural alternative suggestions
  • To improve our service, personalize your experience, and ensure secure access
  • To manage premium subscriptions and payment processing
  • To send important service updates and safety notifications
  • To comply with legal requirements and protect user safety

Health Data Protection

We NEVER:

  • Sell your health information to pharmaceutical companies
  • Share individual medication data with advertisers
  • Use your health data for marketing without consent
  • Store medication photos with personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services:

We use trusted third-party services to operate Naturinex:

  • Firebase (Google): Hosting, authentication, and secure data storage
  • Stripe: Payment processing (we don't store credit card details)
  • OpenAI / Google Gemini: AI suggestion generation (anonymized data only)
  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics

These services have their own privacy policies and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Data Security:

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access controls for employees
  • Compliance with healthcare data protection standards

Data Retention:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data removed within 30 days
  • Anonymized Data: May be retained indefinitely for service improvement
  • Medication Photos: Automatically deleted after processing unless saved by user

Your Rights:

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and marketing communications
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data

Children's Privacy:

Naturinex is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers:

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA):

California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared or sold.

Changes to This Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
